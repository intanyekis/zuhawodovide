Bang Si-hyuk created and now serves as the CEO of Hybe (formerly Big Hit Entertainment), one of Korea’s largest music labels and entertainment businesses.

By listing the company in October 2020, the biggest initial public offering (IPO) in South Korea in the past three years, he became a millionaire. In October 2022, he donated 5 billion won (approximately $3.5 million) to the Community Chest of Korea.

The entertainment industry and his career as a self-made entrepreneur are his main sources of wealth. Forbes estimates that Bang Si-net Hyuk will be worth $2 billion by the year 2023. Bloomberg estimated Bang’s wealth to be above $770 million in April 2019.

The BigHit IPO, which was the largest to be filed in South Korea in three years, lifted Bang’s net worth to $2.8 billion. Bang was now the only billionaire in the nation’s entertainment industry and the sixth-richest person overall as a result.

In June 2021, Forbes Asia published its annual list of the 50 richest people in Korea. Bang was one of just two newcomers to the ranking and was ranked as the 16th richest person in South Korea.

According to the Bloomberg Billionaires Index, Bang is expected to be worth $3.2 billion as of July 2021. Right now, he lives a comfortable life.

Where was Bang Si-Hyuk born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Bang Si-hyuk was born to their father Bang Geuk-Yoon and mother Choi Myung-ja in South Korea on August 9, 1972. His father previously held the position of president of the Korea Workers’ Compensation and Welfare Company at the Korea Social Insurance Research Institute.

He also has a younger sister. In 2018, Choi Kyu-sik, a politician and former journalist, was appointed as the South Korean ambassador to Hungary. The largest mobile gaming company in South Korea, Netmarble, was created by his cousin, Bang Jun-hyuk.

Bang Si-hyuk is South Korean, as shown by his nationality and ethnicity. He cut the cake on his 50th birthday in 2022.

Bang’s family was musical, therefore he was captivated by music from an early age. On the other hand, his parents discouraged him from pursuing a career in the industry. In middle school, Bang formed a band with a few friends.

He began writing songs and performing them. He attended Kyunggi High School before graduating from Seoul National University with a degree in aesthetics.

How did Bang Si-Hyuk start his Professional Career?

Bang debuted as a composer while still a student. He and Park Jin-young started working together to write songs.

He joined him as a producer, arranger, and composer after Park founded JYP Entertainment. One of their first triumphs was “g.o.d,” a band from the first generation. Instruments and music for The g.o.d’s debut album “Chapter 1” were created by Bang. Bang has arranged two of g.o.d’s most well-known songs, “One Candle” and “Road.” At this point, he started using the stage name “Hitman.”

In addition to g.o.d, he has collaborated with a variety of artists, including veteran singers Im Chang-Jung and Park Ji-Yoon, singer-actor Rain, groups Wonder Girls, 2 AM, and Teen Top, and R&B singer Baek Ji-young. He disbanded JYP Entertainment in 2005 to launch Big Hit Entertainment, his own company.

Bang Si-Hyuk continued there to write, compose, and produce for Big Hit artists. He co-wrote six songs for BTS’ critically praised “Wings” album from 2016.

He was on the cover of the April 2022 edition of Time alongside BTS.

Later the same month, he was awarded an honorary doctorate in business from Seoul National University (SNU) in appreciation of his “contribution to the development and innovation of the country’s cultural and entertainment industry” and for his “role in making K-pop into a global pop music culture.” He is the first person in the entertainment sector to be given such a distinction.
