Alejandro Gómez is an international Argentine professional footballer. His full name is Alejandro Darío Gómez. He is most known as Papu Gomez in the football world.

He has played in the Sevilla and the Argentina national football team. Generally, his playing position is Forward, winger, and attacking midfielder.

He played for the national side in Argentina and the under-20 team. On 13 June 2017, he made his international professional debut in the Argentina national team.

Lucas Ocampos, Alexis Mac Allister, Marcos Senesi, Germán Pezzella, Gonzalo Montiel, Juan Musso, and Messi played with him in the Argentina national football team.

Gomez has married his long-time girlfriend Linda Raff. They have three children, a daughter Constantina, and two sons Bautista Gomez and Milo Gomez.

Papu Gomez’s Club, and other careers

Gómez started his football career with the Argentine side club Arsenal de Sarandí in 2003.

In 2009, Gomez joined with Argentine club San Lorenzo de Almagro and his contract fee was 2 million dollars.

On 21 July 2010, Gomez signed and made his debut against Greek club Iraklis. Gomez made a four-year deal with Metalist Kharkiv and his transfer fee was €12 million on 2 August 2013.

Gómez joined in club Atalanta for a three-year contract in the summer season of 2014. On 26 January 2021, Gomez signed a contract until June 2024 with La Liga club Sevilla.

His International, and FIFA 22 career

In the South American Youth Championship 2007, Gomez was named to the Argentina U-20 team.

He was part of Argentina’s under-20 team and won the FIFA U-20 World Cup in 2007.

On 19 May 2017, he was called up to Argentina’s senior squad to play a friendly match against Singapore and Brazil.

In June 2021, Gomez was part of Argentina’s senior for the tour of Copa América 2021.

He was called up to the World Cup 2022 Qatar in the 26-man squad of the Argentine team as an Attacking Midfielder.

After, the Argentine team reached the quarter-final to win round 16 of the World Cup 2022 against the Australian football team (2-1) on 3rd December 2022.
