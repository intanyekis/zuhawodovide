Why Chrishell Stause's Relationhip With G Flip Is Special

 Posted on August 2, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1064 words  |  Martina Birk

Chrishell Stause came out in a new relationship in May 2022 with musician G Flip and their relationship is the real deal. Everyone was shocked to learn the news, especially Stause’s Selling Sunset co-stars as they were kept in the dark before the reveal. People were specifically surprised because of Chrishell Stause’s desire to start a family as soon as possible, which ultimately caused her breakup with Jason Oppenheim.

While that desire still lives strong in Stause, she has spoken out about how G Flip has opened her mind to more possibilities. She explained on Selling Sunset’s reunion episode how there are other ways to have children and love can come from anywhere, any time. Stause and G Flip are defying age gaps and gender norms, so let’s get into exactly what makes them so special.

8 Who Is G Flip?

Fans have a lot of questions about Chrishell Stause’s new partner. G Flip, whose full name is Georgia Flipo. They are an extremely talented musician from Australia and play many instruments as well as sing. They are most known for their songs “About You,” “Drink Too Much,” and “GET ME OUTTA HERE.”

G Flip is non-binary and goes by they/them pronouns. Stause stated on Selling Sunset that she is attracted to masculine energy and that she is drawn to the personality of someone rather than their physical appearance. G Flip's energy clearly fit the bill for Stause as they are now happily in a relationship.

7 How Did G Flip And Chrishell Meet?

G Flip and Stause met on set. Flipo cast Chrishell in their most recent music video for “GET ME OUTTA HERE.” Stause spoke on Selling Sunset about how excited she was to act again after taking such a long break from soap operas. The two got hot and heavy while filming, so it is no surprise their chemistry grew into something more.

The video and song are now streaming on all platforms, giving viewers an inside peak into their beginning. Their public displays of affection continue on their Instagram pages and in adorable paparazzi photos. These two can't seem to get enough of each other!

6 How Old Is Georgia Flipo (And How Old Is Chrishell)?

G Flip is only 27! This is a big change for Chrishell Stause. Previously, Stause had only dated people closer to her age, such as Justin Hartley and Jason Oppenheim. As a 40-year-old woman, Stause is at a very different stage in her life than G Flip. Stause is looking to settle down and have a family, and fans worry G Flip is still enjoying their roaring twenties. However, this large age gap does not seem to affect the happy couple. After all, age is just a number. The two appear hopelessly in love and on the same page with what they want.

5 Chrishell And Justin Hartley Split In 2021

Chrishell Stause’s famous break-up was in the public eye when Justin Hartley filed for divorce back in November 2019. Selling Sunset showcased the incident and its fallout, which was just as shocking to Stause as it was to fans of the couple.

Fans were very surprised to learn that Hartley did not give Stause any warning before filing for divorce and sharing the news with TMZ. Hartley seems to have completely moved on from the split and does not have anything to say about Stause or her new relationship. He is keeping his feelings and new life private.

4 Jason Oppenheim Supports Chrishell Stause And G Flip

Fans of Selling Sunset were heartbroken to hear of Chrishell Stause and Jason Oppenheim’s split at the end of season 5 of the show. Co-stars were rooting for them to end up together, but Oppenheim’s decision to not have children resulted in their breakup.

Oppenheim was one of the first to show support for Stause and G Flip and has continued to stand by Stause’s side. He only wants happiness for Chrishell. Exes should take a page out of his supportive playbook! Other co-stars such as Mary Fitzgerald and Davina Potratz have also spoken out in support of their relationship.

3 Chrishell Announced Relationship With G Flip On Selling Sunset

In Selling Sunset’s reunion episode in May 2022, Stause announced her new relationship with G Flip. The relationship was previously kept secret, even from Stause’s close friends on Selling Sunset.

Maya Vander’s reaction to the news was priceless but full of love for her friend's newfound happiness. Stause was immediately met with overwhelming support from her fellow cast mates, as well as fans on social media. Stause has since taken to Instagram to further share her experience and give eager viewers a little more context on her relationship with G Flip.

2 Chrishell And G Flip Share A Home

Viewers got to watch Chrishell Stause’s process of buying a home last year on Selling Sunset. Her fellow cast mate Amanza Smith helped her decorate the space and turn it into Stause’s dream home. Now, Stause gets to share her home with someone she loves.

After the couple posted multiple photos and videos to social media in Stause’s home, rumors circulated that the two were sharing the beautiful house in the hills. A source told People that “Chrishell moved them in.” The two are making a life together, and fans could not be happier about it.

1 Does G Flip Want A Family With Chrishell Stause?

It is no secret Chrishell Stause wants kids. She ended her relationship with Jason Oppenheim because of her desire to start a family. Since dating G Flip, Stause has been open about having children in other ways such as adoption and very vocal about how this relationship does not prevent her from having children.

Despite their age, G Flip is on board with having kids. They spoke in an episode of the People Every Day Podcast about children, saying “I definitely see children in my future.” They also pointed out that “two people who have the same sex organs can still have children,” so adoption might be in the couple’s future!
