Inside Rebel Wilson's blissful love life: The actress came out of the closet last year!  

Since her rise to stardom with the 2011 comedy film, Bridesmaid, Rebel Wilson become Hollywood's go-to actress to play irreverent characters, not to mention, irrevocably hilarious. Wilson's acclaimed performances in Bachelorette, Pitch Perfect, Isn't It Romantic, and Super Fun Night only added to the typecast.  

Born on March 2, 1980, in Sydney, New South Wales, Wilson started her acting journey in her native. It was there in the land down under where she also kicked off her career as a writer, producer, comedian, and singer.  

The world has witnessed a massive transformation of Rebel in recent years; a transcendence of her public image, resulting from Rebel's venture into more serious roles, and also, the actress revealing herself as a member of the LGBT. The recent shift also constitutes the actress embracing motherhood.  

Who is Rebel Wilson Dating?  

Wilson spent a portion of her life looking for Mr. Perfect, only to realize that she was meant to be with Miss Perfect. The Pitch Perfect actress revealed, what we assume is her first same-sex relationship experience, her romance with Ramona Agruma, a designer, in May 2022.   

"I thought I was searching for a Disney Prince, but maybe what I needed all this time was a Disney princess," Wilson wrote as she shared a loved-up picture of her and Ramona. Since their surprise Insta-official status, Rebel and Ramona have only grown stronger and more adorable as a couple.  

The duo kicked off their exciting journey with a romantic getaway to Italy in June, then enjoyed the U.S. Open to cheer for the national treasure Serena Williams, and enthusiastically commemorated their first Halloween together, all while sharing the bits and pieces of their shenanigans on the Instagram.   

The Bridesmaid actress and her designer girlfriend took their relationship a step further with the launch of their joint business, R & R Club, an exclusive capsule collection of loungewear. Wilson's 2022 journey came to a close with one more enthralling announcement.  

The actress-turned-businesswoman had welcomed a daughter, via surrogacy, by the year's end, and couldn't be more thrilled about it. "I can't even describe the love I have for her, she's a beautiful miracle," wrote the actress in a lengthy, emotional caption. The couple has been candid about their motherhood journey.   

"It's like you start thinking about someone else instead of yourself, priorities changes," said Wilson's girlfriend while dishing on her life after the arrival of the baby. She adds, "It's truly life-changing but in a good way."    

Wilson proposed to Ramona at Disneyland! 

The duo sparked engagement rumors more than one-time last year and after denying the speculation several times, the couple finally got engaged earlier this year. The proposal took place at California's Disneyland and It was reportedly Wilson who got down her knees. The newly engaged are allegedly planning an intimate ceremony.  

Get to Know Rebel Wilson's Fiancée, Ramona Agruma!  

Wilson herself is an all-rounder A-lister in Hollywood and her soon-to-be wife, Agruma, is not entirely unrelated to the showbiz circle. Agruna runs a high-end jewelry brand called, DeLys, which boasts a celebrity clientele. In addition, Agruma also serves as the brand ambassador for the Turkish jewelry company, Bee Goddess.   

Ramona is also the founder of Lemon Ve Limon, a sustainable fashion company. It is no secret that despite her extensive business feats, Agruma is better known for her fairy tale romance with the Pitch Perfect actress. The entrepreneur previously opened up about how navigating her personal life in the spotlight has been a new and unfamiliar experience. 

Rebel Wilson's Love Life Before Ramona Agruma!  

Rebel has had her share of romances, some unpleasant ones, throughout her career in the industry. The Senior Year actress once dished on how she put up with things that she wouldn't have in her past relationships. The Australian actress's first relationship in the spotlight happens to be with actor-producer Mickey Gooch Jr.   

The pair's brief romance began after they met on the sets of How to Be Single. Wilson next dated stuntman Aden Stay for a brief period in 2017. After two low-key romances, the Hustle actress hit it big for the first time with businessman Jacob Busch.  

Wilson and Busch's on-off relationship consisted of them going official, making their red-carpet debut, traveling across the world, and finally, announcing their breakup in early 2021. "Single girl headed to Super Bowl," Wilson wrote as she confirmed her breakup with Busch.
