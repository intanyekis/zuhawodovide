Highlights

  • Elsina Khayrova may seek revenge on Tom Cruise after their breakup, with her ex-husband warning Cruise about her expensive tastes.
  • The relationship reportedly ended due to concerns about Elsina's lavish spending habits, prompting Cruise to cut ties despite their public debut.
  • Elsina has potential to reveal personal secrets about Cruise for the right price, including details about his past relationships and work in Scientology.

Tom Cruise recently found himself single again when he broke up with his girlfriend Elsina Khayrova, but she’s apparently not out of his life completely. According to a new report, the Russian socialite is keen on getting “revenge” against Cruise for the break-up.

"She followed every one of Tom's demands," a source claimed to the National Enquirer. "She was very discreet about their relationship and didn't push things."

"Then, because of [her ex-husband] Dmitry, Tom gets paranoid and jettisons her. It was just so unfair and cruel!" they added.

Elsina’s Ex-Husband Warned Tom To Be Careful

Tom and Elsina’s break-up came shortly after her ex-husband, Dmitry Tsetkov, issued a cautionary message to Cruise during an interview with Daily Mail, advising him he’d need to spend a lot to keep up with Elsina’s luxurious taste, saying he’d need to "keep his wallet wide open" given her preference for the "expensive and luxurious."

According to Tsetkov, during their relationship, Elsina, 36, spent approximately $12 million on clothing items and a staggering $2 million on handbags alone.

“Irrespective of whoever she’s with, Tom Cruise or anybody else, they should be aware that she likes the finer things in life,” he went on . “I’m happy for her, I wish her all the best.”

“I haven’t spoken with Elsina about this because our preferred method of communication is through our lawyers. She’s 36, she’s beautiful, financially independent and loves life,” the Russian oligarch added.

This revelation may explain why Cruise was so quick to end things with Elsina, despite publicly debuting their relationship. He may have begun feeling the weight of the relationship on his bank account, or was at least fearing what her ex-husband warned him of.

Elsina Is Reportedly Interested In Spilling Tom’s Secrets

Now, the National Enquirer warn that Elsina’s love for the luxurious life ins’t all Tom has to worry about, as she may be persuaded to open up about their relationship and his personal life, especially if the price is right.

"He confided in her about everything, from his past relationships to his Scientology work and more," the insider went on. "If she does talk, it's going to be a total nightmare for him!"

Though reports suggested Tom saw a future with Elsina, it’s apparently over between the two, though things could get messy. The Mission: Impossible actor has been married thrice before.

His first marriage was to actress Mimi Rogers in 1987, but they divorced in 1990. Cruise then married Nicole Kidman in 1990, and they adopted two children, Isabella and Connor, during their marriage, which ended in 2001.

Following his divorce from Kidman, Cruise married actress Katie Holmes in 2006. They welcomed a daughter, Suri Cruise, in the same year. However, Cruise and Holmes separated in 2012 and finalized their divorce in a matter of weeks the same year. The actor has allegedly had no contact with Suri since their divorce, though he pays a high price in child support and will reportedly financially support her when she attends college later this year.
