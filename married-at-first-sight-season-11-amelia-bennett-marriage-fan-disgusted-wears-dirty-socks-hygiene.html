'Married At First Sight' Season 11 premiered this week and the fans are already talking about the quirks and flaws that make up these couples. Amelia and Bennett have emerged as one of the most adorable pairs. And viewers have predicted that they will stay married if they work on their relationship while implying that they also need to take a good look at their questionable hygiene.

"Do we think hygiene is important to Amelia and Bennett?" a fan asked online. Amelia, while trying on wedding dresses, accidentally (or otherwise) showed her armpit hair. She was okay and appeared relaxed about it, but fans took it to a whole new level. They are shocked beyond belief that Amelia would do something like that on national television even if it happened unwittingly.

Her match Bennett was severely criticized by fans because of the dirty socks he wore to his wedding. They also had issues with his unkempt hair and wished if someone told him how much of a mess it was. Maybe that's his style? At times, viewers can be unforgiving towards the characters they see on television because of how passionate they are about them and only want the best for them.

Meanwhile, here's what the fans have to say about Amelia's armpit hair and Bennett's dirty socks.

"Oh wow Amelia. The show should have had a warning for Amelia wearing strapless. I wasn't prepared for those armpits. They sneak attacked me," a viewer commented, while another wrote: "I literally only jumped on Twitter to see if anyone’s chatting about Amelias armpit hair #MAFS #MarriedAtFirstSight."

"Those armpits though. They are going to need a lawnmower to shave them. #MarriedAtFirstSight #MAFS," a user tweeted.

"Bennett.... I’d rather you not wear socks than wear dirty disgusting ones.... #mafs #marriedatfirstsight," a viewer expressed, while another asked: "Soooo Bennett didn’t have any clean socks? #MarriedAtFirstSight."

"Bennett, you have shitty friends, because they are allowing you to wear those socks! Also, why did you bring dirty socks? I’m sure there’s a Walgreens across the street! #MarriedAtFirstSight #MAFS," another fan posted on social media.
