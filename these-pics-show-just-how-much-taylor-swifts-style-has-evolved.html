These Pics Show Just How Much Taylor Swift's Style Has Evolved

 Posted on August 2, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  924 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

When Taylor Swift rose to fame in the mid-2000s no one could expect that in the upcoming decade the musician would become one of the most successful stars in the music industry. In 2021, however, Taylor Swift is one of the most talked-about pop artists and her fame doesn’t seem to be going anywhere any time soon.

Related: Here's How Miley Cyrus' Style Has Evolved

Today’s list takes a look at Taylor Swift's style and just how much it has evolved over the years. From her signature tiny curls and sparkly dresses to toning down her style and rocking messy bangs — keep on scrolling to see just what Taylor Swift's style journey looked like so far!

10 Let's Go All The Way Back To 2004 — When Taylor Was 14 And Rocking Curly Hair

We are kicking the list off with the year 2004 when Taylor Swift was 14 years old and an upcoming country star that most people haven’t heard of yet. As seen from the photos above, back then Taylor rocked her signature tiny curls and some classic 2000 fashion which included plenty of brightly colored off-the-shoulder tops!

9 By 2006 Taylor's Most Well-Known Accessory Was Her Guitar

In 2006 Taylor Swift released her debut single "Tim McGraw" from her self-titled debut album and with that she quickly rose to stardom. At the time, Taylor's go-to looks always included very defined curls and of course her guitar. The musician's outfits were always very girly and chic and at the time the star was almost never taking any fashion risks.

8 2008 Was All About Side-Swept Curls And Sparkly Dresses

In the next couple of years, Taylor Swift's style certainly changed a bit — but at the end of the day, Taylor Swift in 2008 was still all about curly hair and girly dresses.

Related: Harry Styles' 10 Best Stage Outfits

As seen from the photos above, Taylor did like rocking dark eyeliner but it was always in combination with lots of sparkly jewelry or a full-on sparkly dress!

7 And Two Years Later The Sparkly Dresses Were Still There — But The Curls Were Gone

By the end of the 2000s, Taylor Swift's style started changing a bit more and while the musician — who is known for having plenty of famous friends in her inner circle — did still enjoy wearing pretty dresses, she would often ditch the signature curls and opt for an edgier rocker-chic straight hairstyle, as seen in the photos above. Little did fans know at the time that Taylor would soon explore even edgier looks.

6 In 2012 Taylor Was All About Hats And High Waisted Shorts

In 2012 Taylor Swift released her fourth studio album Red which included plenty of hits such as "We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together" and "I Knew You Were Trouble", and during that era, the musician definitely had a very distinct style. Yes — when fans think of Taylor Swift's Red era, sparkly tops, hats, and high-waisted shorts certainly immediately come to mind!

5 In 2014 Taylor Rocked A Short Bob And Plenty Of Matching Sets

In 2014 Taylor Swift — who was linked to quite a few famous celebs over the years — released her fifth studio album 1989, and one thing that was common when it comes to her outfits at the time is that the musician rocked plenty of matching sets. Yes, as seen in the photos above — Taylor also cut her hair to a shoulder-length bob which certainly suited the musician extremely well!

4 And Two Years Later The Pop Star Even Gave Us Some Edginess

As most of Taylor Swift's fans will agree, 2016 was a year in which Taylor gave us some of her most edgy looks as the star was rocking a super short bob haircut at the time — and she certainly experimented with different kinds of outfits and makeup.

Related: Kendall Jenner's 10 Best Steet Fashion Looks

As seen from the photos above, Taylor even opted to go platinum blonde and rock black lipstick — something that no one could imagine the young musician doing in 2004!

3 Which She Explored Even More In 2017

In 2017 Taylor released her sixth studio album Reputation and by then it was evident that Taylor does not care what others think of her anymore. The musician became fiercely independent and would speak her mind whenever she could — and when it comes to fashion Taylor wore whatever she liked regardless of what others may think! Taylor's Reputation era certainly gave us some of her most iconic stage outfits!

2 In 2019 Taylor Decided To Rock Bold Colors

Taylor's seventh studio album Lover was released in 2019 and with its release, Taylor certainly decided to embrace colors more often. As seen from the photos above, rocking boring monochromatic looks was definitely not something Taylor enjoyed as the star certainly developed a love for funky cuts and colorful materials! While Taylor's style did change, her lyrics remained just as relatable.

1 And Today The Musician Is All About Simplicity

And finally — wrapping the list up is the musician's current style. With having released two albums in 2020 — Folklore and Evermore — Taylor Swift's style certainly toned down a lot, and now the musician is very much into wearing super simple looks. If you judge Taylor Swift by her current outfits you could certainly never tell that the star has a $400 million fortune!
