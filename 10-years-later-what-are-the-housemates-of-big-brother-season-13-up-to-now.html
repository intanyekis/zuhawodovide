10 Years Later: What Are The Housemates Of 'Big Brother' Season 13 Up To Now?

 Posted on June 7, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  885 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Cast your minds back to a decade ago. Katy Perry and Bruno Mars were topping the charts, hair feathers and skull-print scarves were all the rage... and the word 'pandemic' wasn't even in our vocabulary. Big Brother began its thirteenth season in the summer of 2011 on CBS. This season welcomed eight brand new houseguests, as well as six returning contestants from previous seasons.

The show entertained viewers with several twists over ten weeks. All new houseguests were partnered into duos and the returning 'veteran' houseguests were labeled 'Dynamic Duos'. The contestants were awarded a 'Golden Key' if their partner was evicted, which granted them immunity. This season also saw the return of the 'Pandora's Box', which contained either punishment or reward.

10 Jordan Lloyd

Jordan was a returning houseguest from Big Brother 11, which she won. She ended up finishing at fourth place two seasons later in Big Brother 13.

Now living out in Colorado, Jordan married fellow contestant, Jeff Schroeder in 2016. The couple has two sons, Lawson Keith born in 2016, and Layton Sarti born in 2018.

9 Porsche Briggs

A cocktail waitress from Miami Beach, Florida, Porsche was one of the new houseguests and became the runner-up.  She formed a close friendship with the winner, Rachel during the show, but this didn't last after the first eviction of Rachel's fiance, Brendon.

Porsche has recently been involved in a fundraising event, Hearts of Reality. She shared the information about the event on her Instagram account last year, which was benefitting the organization, Give Kids The World.

8 Adam Poch

Adam was a music inventory manager from East Brunswick, New Jersey. He was also a new houseguest, who went on to finish in third place when Rachel cast the sole vote to evict him.

On Adam's Instagram account, he describes himself as a "Food Judge" and "Ordained Minister". He also shares regular photos of his daughter, Natalie Victoria, who was born in 2015.

7 Rachel Reilly

Rachel was a returning houseguest from the previous season, Big Brother 12, who went on to win Big Brother 13. She entered with her then-fiance, Brendon Villegas, who she also met as a fellow houseguest from the previous season.

She married Brendon in 2012 and now the couple has two children: a girl named Adora Borealis, born in 2016, and a boy named Adler, born in 2020.

6 Kalia Booker

Also one of the 'newbies', Kalia made it to fifth place in the thirteenth season before being evicted. The writer from Philadelphia described herself as "dramatic, adventurous and gregarious".

Since then, Kalia seems to have taken a step back from the spotlight and her Instagram account is private (although she is followed by Queer Eye's Karamo Brown!). She also attended the Big Brother/The Price Is Right crossover show in 2016, but didn't compete.

5 Shelly Moore

Another 'newbie', Shelly made it to sixth place before being evicted from the house. The 41-year-old Outdoors Industry Executive was partnered with Cassi Colvin and then Adam Poch during the show.

Shelly describes herself as a "Mom, wife, believer" in her Instagram bio and shares photos of her lifestyle and family with her followers regularly.

4 Jeff Schroeder

Jeff was one of the returning houseguests, as he had previously taken part in Big Brother 11, where he met his now-wife, Jordan. He made it to seventh place in Big Brother 13.

As well as being married to Jordan and being a father to the couple's two sons, Jeff is also hosting for Daily Blast Live. He is also featured on a Youtube channel with his wife, called Jeff and Jordan TV.

3 Daniele Donato

A regular feature of the show, Daniele was the runner-up of Big Brother 8, came eighth in Big Brother 13, and returned to Big Brother All-Stars in 2020, where she finished in seventh place. Daniele is also the daughter of Big Brother 8 winner, Dick Donato, with whom she was partnered this season before he walked out.

Daniele met her now-husband, Dominic Briones on the show and they were married in 2013. The couple now has a daughter together, Autumn Tennessee, who was born in 2018. Several heart-warming snaps of her family can be found on the star's Instagram account.

2 Brendon Villegas

Brendon met Big Brother 13 winner, Rachel, in the previous season, when the couple shared an on-screen 'showmance'. Despite his now wife's success, Brendon only placed ninth in this season.

Aside from sharing adorable photos of his family, including the couple's adorable new baby boy, his Instagram shows that he is a Biomedical Physicist and "Lung Cancer Eradicator". It also proudly states that he is a "World traveler", husband and father.

1 Lawon Exum

Lawon came in tenth place, after a unanimous 6-0 vote to evict him, upon his own request. He was hoping to return with "special powers", but this never happened.

According to his Twitter page, Lawon is living in LA. It states that he attended USC Annenberg '21 and is now a journalist, involved with the organization, the National Association of Black Journalists.
