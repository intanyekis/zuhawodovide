Rapper 6ix9ines baby mama speaks out about his recent gym attack

 Posted on May 31, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  190 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Sara Molina, the mother of rapper Tekashi 6ix9ine’s 7-year-old daughter, has claimed that the recent brutal gym attack on the rapper has left their daughter feeling “embarrassed.”

Molina, who shares 7-year-old Saraiyah with the rapper, revealed to TMZ that her ex’s ongoing behavior has caused embarrassment for both her and their daughter.

The rapper, whose real name is Daniel Hernandez, was hospitalized after being assaulted by “three or four thugs” in an LA Fitness bathroom in Florida.

Molina expressed disappointment, stating that their daughter will eventually become aware of her father’s actions.

She criticized the 26-year-old performer for not having security with him, especially after a recent incident at the World Baseball Classic.

Molina expressed concerns about their daughter’s safety due to the rapper’s ongoing feuds with other artists.

She also claimed that the rapper has not been in touch with their daughter since late last year.

The “Fefe” performer also has a 3-year-old daughter, Briella, and his relationship with her has raised issues and controversies in the past.

Following the recent attack, there are reports that the rapper has no intentions of increasing his security or leaving Florida.
