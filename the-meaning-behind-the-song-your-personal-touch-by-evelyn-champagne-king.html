The Meaning Behind The Song: Your Personal Touch by Evelyn Champagne King

 Posted on July 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  560 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Whenever I listen to the song “Your Personal Touch” by Evelyn “Champagne” King, it brings back fond memories of my younger days. This vibrant and energetic track, which was released in 1985 as the lead single from Evelyn Champagne King’s album “A Long Time Coming (A Change Is Gonna Come),” holds a special place in my heart. The catchy beats, powerful vocals, and heartfelt lyrics make it a timeless classic.

The song’s chorus, “Come on and get my love, your personal touch,” immediately grabs your attention and sets the tone for the entire track. It speaks about the desire for an intimate connection with someone, a touch that is uniquely their own. The repetition of the line emphasizes the longing for this personal touch and the eagerness to receive it.

The Power of Love

The lyrics of the song describe the transformative power of love and its ability to turn everything into gold. “[Verse 1] Everything that you touch in my life just turns to gold,” Evelyn Champagne King sings. This line captures the idea that love has the ability to enrich our lives and bring immense joy and happiness.

Furthermore, the song highlights the profound impact that a particular person’s love can have on an individual’s life. It emphasizes the fact that no one else can love or understand you the way that this person does. “[Verse 1] No one ever, never love me, baby, the way you do,” King sings. This sentiment speaks to the deep emotional connection and the unique bond shared between two individuals.

The Thrill of Love

The pre-chorus of the song delves deeper into the exhilaration and excitement that love brings. The lyrics express the anticipation of experiencing love’s embrace every night. “[Pre-Chorus] Ooh, your loving is so right, and I want it every night,” King sings. This line perfectly captures the intensity of desire and the longing for the physical and emotional presence of a loved one.

Moreover, the lyrics speak to the admiration and admiration for the person who possesses this personal touch. King celebrates the uniqueness and class of her lover, emphasizing how their love is worth her while. “[Pre-Chorus] You got class and you got style, and your love is worth my while,” she sings. This appreciation for the partner’s qualities adds depth to the song, showcasing not only the physical attraction but also the admiration for their personality.

A Timeless Message

“Your Personal Touch” by Evelyn Champagne King is a testament to the enduring power of love and the importance of meaningful connections. The lyrics evoke feelings of longing, desire, and devotion, reminding us of the beauty and impact of love in our lives.

This song resonates with me because it reminds me of the importance of cherishing the personal touch shared with loved ones. As I listen to this track, I am transported back to moments spent with family, friends, and my significant other, dancing and singing along to its catchy melody.

“Your Personal Touch” is a testament to the timeless nature of Evelyn Champagne King’s music and the universal experiences of love and connection. It serves as a reminder to appreciate and recognize the significance of the personal touch in our own lives.
