Kaleth Morales Wikipedia

Kaleth Morales Wikipedia: Kaleth Morales was born on 9 June 1984 in Valledupar, Cesar, Colombia. He was a Colombian vallenato singer and songwriter. Having put out singles like Vivo en el Limbo, he was well known as the founder of the "Nueva Ola" movement in Vallenato. Kaleth wrote a number of popular tracks while still a teen that was recorded by other bands. He collaborated with accordionist Manuel Julián to record "Voy an Atraparte," his debut song, in 2003. In 2005, the CD "La Hora de la Verdad" was made available. The hits from the album, "Vivo En El Limbo" and "La Hora de la Verdad," were distributed across Venezuela and Ecuador, where they are still well-liked today.
