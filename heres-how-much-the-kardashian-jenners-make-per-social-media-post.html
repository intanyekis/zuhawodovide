Here's How Much The Kardashian-Jenners Make Per Social Media Post

  • Kim Kardashian ( $1,689,000 per post)

  • Kourtney Kardashian ($785,000 per post)
  • Kris Jenner ($188,800 per post)
  • Caitlyn Jenner ($57.4k)
  • Rob Kardashian ($7.9k per post)

 

'Momager' Kris Jenner gets a 10% cut of all the business ventures her daughters promote to their fan followers. For each Instagram/Facebook post and story, they charge more than six figures. Their total followers on Instagram account to over 1.3 billion, and Kylie Jenner is the highest-paid among them for her social media. Kim comes in second when it comes to making millions via one post.

The Kardashian-Jenner family's wealth is estimated at more than $ 2 billion from their brand endorsements alone. Kim earns $1.4 million approximately per Instagram post. Kylie and Kendall Jenner make $1.1 million and $1 million, respectively. Khloe and Kourtney are worth $949,000 and $785,000 per post.

Kylie Jenner ( $1,835,000 per post)

 

The youngest member of the clan is the highest-earning member. Apart from her colossal cosmetic venture Kylie earns millions for promoting lifestyle brands to her 375 million followers. According to HopperHQ, she is among the top three celebrities who make $1,835,000 per post on Instagram. The wealth has doubled with the model promoting voguish beauty and fashion products. In 2016 20% came from endorsing Puma and Pacsun on social media. This accounted for over $18 million extra in earnings. Luxury items from Calvin Klein, Adidas, Beats x Balmain, and Skims are highly recommended to her fans by the beauty mogul.

Kim Kardashian ( $1,689,000 per post)

 

The Queen Bee of the clan has recently attained $1 billion status in 2022 with her total net worth. Her 336 million followers have placed the model as the sixth highest-paid celebrity on social media in 2022. Kim is seen promoting her products like Skims, KKW Beauty & Fragrance, Kimoji, and Kardashian beauty. According to USMagazine, the socialite herself claims to make more money via a single post on Instagram than an entire KUWTK season.

Kendall Jenner ( $1,290,000 per post)

 

The leggy beauty was the world's highest-paid supermodel in 2017. She doesn’t miss a beat when updating her social feed with the latest fashion statements. Her 266 million followers love to take style inspirations from each post. Kendall earns approximately $1,290,000 via Instagram promotions. For a single tweet, Jenner is known to charge $300,000. All coveted brands like Pepsi, Estee Lauder, Adidas, and Stuart Weitzman have collaborated with Victoria’s Secret models in millions.

Khloe Kardashian ($1,320,000 per post)

 

The founder of the Good America clothing brand is among the top ten social influencers worldwide to earn six figures. Khloe has a loyal fan following of 282 million, for which she makes approximately $1,320,000 million per endorsement post. According to DailyMail, she is more of a body positivity campaigner. Fashion brands like Balenciaga and Shein have worked with the fashion icon. Her home brands, Skims, Good America, and KKW Beauty, have been an eternal source of income through social media pitches.

Kourtney Kardashian ($785,000 per post)

 

The vegan Kardashian is known to promote health and wellness through her social posts. She is the least-followed member of the Kardashian-Jenner clan, with 205 million followers. Poosh is a balanced lifestyle brand founded by her that shares fine-dining healthy recipes, fashion tips, and beauty secrets. The entrepreneur makes around $ 785,000 per post based on these themes.

Kris Jenner ($188,800 per post)

 

The family's matriarch is known to earn 10% from all her children's business ventures. Kris is seen promoting holiday sales, fashion accessories, and clothes to her 51 million followers. It is estimated that she earns around $188,800 per post. According to SCMP, her net worth is approximately $190 million, with KUWTK being the primary source of income.

Caitlyn Jenner ($57.4k)

 

According to Wonderwall, the Olympic-winning media personality charges a whopping $2,300 for a quick hello to her fans via video call. For charity-related posts, Caitlyn charges around $10 -15,000 while promoting them to her 14 million followers. Her posts are primarily family-oriented, so her estimated earnings are far less than the other family members.

Rob Kardashian ($7.9k per post)

 

The man-of-the-house Rob, has turned his social media into a lucrative business by launching his hot sauce brand Grandeza. With over 3 million followers, the former reality star earns around $7.9k for promoting his business online. His sock collection Arthur George is another venture that brings in additional income. Privacy is the top priority for the full-time dad, but that doesn’t mean staying away from being business savvy in the virtual world.

The Kardashian-Jenner clan has turned their social media pages into lucrative business deals with brands. These highly successful women have endorsed everything from weight loss products to fashion. Their billion-dollar empire continues to grow along with their fan following.
