NeNe Leakes Walks Out of RHOA Reunion After Kenya Moore Fight: Watch

 Posted on June 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  325 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Sisters stick together! The cast of The Real Housewives of Atlanta has certainly endured their share of drama, but they come together in the third part of their season 7 reunion to comfort a weeping NeNe Leakes as she tries to walk out of the special after an emotional moment with Kenya Moore — and you’ll never guess the ringleader of the group hug.

Leakes and Moore exchanged words after Moore accused the original Atlanta Housewife of flirting with Cynthia Bailey‘s husband, Peter Thomas, and their blowup continues in the final installment of the reunion — but they’re not trading barbs this time. The near-walkout comes after Dr. Jeff returns to work with the Housewives on their issues once again.

Instead of more screaming and fighting like in the previous installments of the reunion, Moore takes a kinder approach when reaching out to her castmate. “NeNe, I think that you think we’re all against you. We have our fights, we have our arguments, things are said,” Moore says. “You feel like we don’t like you; we don’t love you. But we don’t want to be at that same place with you. We want to move on.”

The typical Housewives storm-out includes incensed language and a table flip or two, but Leakes is full-on weeping into the arms of her husband, Gregg Leakes. 

“I don’t want to talk about my mom,” she whispers as Moore tries to bring up the things Leakes has in common with the rest of the Housewives.
