Who Is Les Sneads Wife Kara Henderson?

 Posted on May 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  257 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Kara Henderson is a sports reporter for the NFL Network, but she is also the beautiful and charming wife of Los Angeles Rams general manager Les Snead.

Prior to joining Stan Kroenke’s organization, Les was the Atlanta Falcons’ director of player personnel, where he began his career as a pro-scout.

When Snead worked for the Jacksonville Jaguars in 1995, he held the same job; he began his career as a graduate assistant at Auburn, his beloved mater.

Before we get into Kara, it’s vital to note that Les Snead, born Samuel Lester Snead, has two children from his first marriage to Liz Snead: Logan and Cannon.

In March 2018, Logan, a linebacker from Eufala High School in Alabama, visited Nick Saban in Alabama.

Following Liz and Les’ divorce, he met Kara and married her in 2012.

Kara was born on June 27, 1973, and she was previously married to Dale Voorhees Haver, the father of her son Tate, just like her famous husband. In September 2011, they filed for divorce.

Mrs. Henderson Snead earned a bachelor’s degree in political science from Duke University before coming to Laude.

She began her career in 1998 with NESN, co-hosting the show Front Row, and later worked as a production assistant at ESPN, ABC News, and CNN.

Kara was also a reporter and anchor for FOX Sports Net New England, as well as a contributor for NFL.com, NFL Network, and Thursday Night Football coverage until 2012 when she retired to focus on her marriage to Les Snead and parenting her son.
