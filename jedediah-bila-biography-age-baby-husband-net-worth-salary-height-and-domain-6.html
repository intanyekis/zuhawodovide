Jedediah Bila Fox News

Jedediah rejoined Fox News as a contributor in November 2018. In April t5he year 2019, she has named a permanent co-host of Fox & Friends Weekend. Bila’s transition from education to politics came in the year 2009, when she reviewed radio host Mark Levin’s book Liberty and Tyranny: A Conservative Manifesto on her blog. Levin later read the review on air, prompting television host Sean Hannity to invite her on his Fox News program, Hannity.

Jedediah joined Fox News as a contributor in 2013. In April the year 2014, she began appearing as a panelist on the daytime talk show Outnumbered. Jedediah also appeared regularly on the talk show The Five.

In August 2016, Jedediah departed Fox News to join the ABC daytime talk show The View as a permanent co-host during its 20th season. While on the show, Bila served as the conservative voice to contrast the liberal co-hosts. In 2017, Jedediah interviewed television personality Abby Lee Miller for the special Abby Tells All on Lifetime. That same year, Jedediah departed The View on September 18.
